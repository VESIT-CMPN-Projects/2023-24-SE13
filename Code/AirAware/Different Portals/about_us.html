About Us

Welcome to AirAware, your go-to source for Air Quality Index (AQI) information. We are dedicated to providing accurate and up-to-date data on air quality and pollutants in cities around the world.

Data Visualization Tools

At AirAware, we utilize interactive graphs to illustrate the levels of pollutants in your city. Our color-coded maps offer a visual representation of AQI values, making it easier for you to understand and assess the air quality in your area.

These tools help us provide comprehensive information on various pollutants and their impact on air quality, empowering individuals to take proactive measures for a healthier environment.

Thank you for choosing AirAware as your trusted resource for AQI information. Together, let's use data visualization to drive positive change and promote better air quality!
